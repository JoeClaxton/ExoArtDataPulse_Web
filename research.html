Research Philosophy

ExoArtDataPulse explores emerging technologies not only as scientific achievements, but as social systems.

Quantum computing, artificial intelligence, advanced sensing technologies and cybersecurity all exist within wider networks of institutions, funding structures, governments, universities, businesses and communities.

The aim is to understand these relationships and make them accessible to a wider audience. The site is intended as a place of orientation for readers earnestly looking for useful signposts in a fast-moving technological landscape.

Research Influences

Current reading and study includes work by C. Wright Mills, Michael Burawoy, Michel Foucault, James C. Scott, Lewis Mumford, Jacques Ellul, Andrew Feenberg, Mariana Mazzucato, Manuel Castells, Scott Aaronson and Douglas Hofstadter.

These influences support a broad approach to technology: scientific, social, institutional, historical and public-facing.
